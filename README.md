# PHII – Preventive Health Intelligence Interface

PHII is an AI-powered preventive health intelligence system designed to identify early health risk signals before symptoms appear.

Instead of reacting to illness, PHII analyzes lifestyle signals and health patterns to generate preventive insights and a PHII health score.

## Problem

In many healthcare systems, especially in developing regions, individuals rarely perform preventive screenings. Most medical attention happens only after symptoms appear, when diseases may already be advanced.

However, people constantly generate health-related signals such as:

- Sleep patterns
- Activity levels
- Medical test history
- Lifestyle behaviors
- Wearable data

This data remains scattered and underutilized.

PHII aims to transform these signals into actionable preventive intelligence.

---

## Solution

PHII collects lifestyle and preventive signals and analyzes them using an AI-driven scoring and signal detection model.

The platform identifies preventive blind spots such as:

- Missing health screenings
- Long gaps in diagnostic tests
- Lifestyle risk patterns
- Age-based preventive windows

It then generates:

- PHII Preventive Health Score
- Preventive Risk Radar
- Health Trajectory Insights
- AI Preventive Intelligence Summary

## PHII Score Concept

The PHII score represents an individual's preventive health readiness.

It evaluates:

- Screening frequency
- Preventive diagnostic coverage
- Lifestyle indicators
- Age-based preventive windows

Higher scores indicate stronger preventive health behavior.
